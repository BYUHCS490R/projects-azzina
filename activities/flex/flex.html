1. What you learned about Flex and CSS.
I learned that flex is like grid but it looks easier to use. Unlike grid that sets a specific layout for where each element goes, flex can adjust according to the screen of the device. With flex you can also reorder items without changing the main layout for smaller devices too.

2. How far did you get on your Flex Box Froggy Game or What did you learn on your own?
I did all 24 rounds in the flexbox froggy game.
