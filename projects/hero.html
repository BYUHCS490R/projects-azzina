My Hero - YOASOBI

YOASOBI is a J-POP duo that first debuted in 2019. Part of the group is Ayase, who is a songwriter and producer, and Lilas Ikuta, a vocalist. Their songs were first based on fictional stories, but now come from different media, like books, messages, and social media posts. Their songs are mostly fast paced, and have complex musical structures varying in different parts of the song.

Top YOASOBI Songs

Yoru ni Kakeru
This was their debut single and stayed for six consecutive weeks on Billboard Japan Hot 100.
Gunjou
This song was based on the manga titled Blue Period and is described as "a cheering song that inspires listeners by immersing themselves in what they like and expressing what they see."
Idol
This song aired as the Opening song to the hit anime and manga "Oshi no Ko". This song set the record for the longest-running number one song on Japan Hot 100 history with 22 non-consecutive weeks.
