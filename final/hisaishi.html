Mamoru Fujisawa, or more commonly known as Joe Hisaishi was born December 6, 1950 in Nagano, Japan. He's a Japanese composer who provided the soundtrack for all of Miyazaki's Ghibli films. His beautiful emotional orchestral pieces were inspired by European classical composers such as Beethoven, Mozart, and Bach.(Ghibli Museum). (Ghibli Wiki, 2026).
