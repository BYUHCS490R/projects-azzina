Hayao Miyazaki was born January 5, 1941 in Tokyo, Japan. He is a studio director, animator, and cartoonist. Throughout his entire career, he has worked under several aliases such as Saburo Akitsu, Tsutomu Teruki, and Miya Iwasaki. He is the co-founder of Studio Ghibli, Chairman of the Tokuma Memorial Animation Cultural Foundation and Mitaka Municipal Animation Museum of Art(Ghibli Museum). (Ghibli Wiki, 2026).
